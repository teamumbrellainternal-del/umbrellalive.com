The music industry grew.

For decades, the music industry evolved around gatekeepers and middlemen. Streaming platforms paid pennies per play. Venues took massive cuts. Booking agents held all the power.

But the artists? They got 13%.

Umbrella exists to change that. No middlemen. No gatekeepers. Just you, your audience, and the tools to build a real career in music.
